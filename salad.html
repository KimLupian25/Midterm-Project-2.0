Buko Salad

Ingredients

  • 4 cups young coconut, shredded
  • 1 can (30 ounces) fruit cocktail or tropical fruit mix, draine
  • 1 can (8 ounces) pineapple tidbits, drained
  • 1 can (15 ounces) sweet corn kernels, drained
  • 1 bottle (12 ounces) kaong, drained
  • 1 bottle (12 ounces) nata de coco, drained
  • 1 cup Eden brand or Velveeta
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (14 ounces) all-purpose cream

Procedure

  1. In a large bowl, combine young coconut, fruit cocktail, pineapple tidbits, corn kernels, kaong, nata de coco, cheese, condensed milk, and all-purpose cream. Stir gently to distribute.
  2. Chill for a few hours or freeze until firm.
