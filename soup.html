Pinoy Vegetable Soups

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons salted butter
  • 1 medium red onion peeled, sliced
  • 1 large carrot peeled, cubed small
  • 1 piece sayote peeled, cubed small
  • 1 medium potato peeled, cubed small
  • 1 liter water
  • 2 pieces chicken bouillon cubes or any flavor as desired
  • 1 154-ml can evaporated milk
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  1. In a small pot over medium heat, add butter. Once butter is melted, add onion, stirring to prevent sticking. Cook until aromatic.
  2. Add carrots, sayote, and potatoes. Stir to combine. Pour in water and add the bouillon cubes. Bring it to a boil and then lower heat to a simmer. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Pour in evaporated milk and stir to combine. Season with salt and ground black pepper.
